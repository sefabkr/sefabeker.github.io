Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

FocusSpace does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. All app data (focus sessions, statistics, theme preferences, etc.) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or advertising identifiers
  • Contacts, photos, files, or any device content

Third-Party Services

FocusSpace does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data brokers. No third parties receive any data from the app.

Children’s Privacy

FocusSpace is suitable for general audiences and does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
